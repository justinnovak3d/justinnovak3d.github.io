Wyndham - Vritual Resorts and Virtual Interiors - Desktop VR

Two different VR Experiences, One taking a customer on a journey across Wyndham Resort locations. The other an internal tool used to view the production of a future resort.

For Wyndham Virtual Resorts I came in later on in the project, once all the environments were scanned. My responsibilities mainly revolved around model and shader optimizations.

For Virtual Interiors I was in charge of creating the space from blueprints working with the Archetects and designers to create the space they invisioned.

Model Cleanup, Custom furniture and room modeling, Texturing, Lighting, Shaders, Animations and Interactions.
